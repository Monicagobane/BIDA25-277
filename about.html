Our story

Aurela began with a simple belief — that fine jewelry should feel personal, not mass-produced. What started as a small custom-order workshop has grown into a full collection spanning rings, necklaces, bracelets and watches, all handcrafted using premium materials.

We work with women who love fashion and fine jewelry, collectors seeking exquisite pieces, and men looking for a timeless watch — while our gold-buying service also serves investors looking to trade.

18k & 24k gold

Sourced responsibly, finished by hand for lasting shine and durability.

Sterling silver

A versatile, affordable option without compromising on finish or detail.

Platinum

Our most durable metal, reserved for statement and heirloom pieces.

Our gold-buying service

Beyond crafting jewelry, Aurela also purchases gold jewelry, coins and bullion directly from investors and collectors. Every item is weighed and valued transparently against current market rates, with no pressure and no hidden fees.
